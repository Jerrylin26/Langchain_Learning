Everything You Need to Know About Gaminin

How does Gaminin ensure product quality?

Every product is meticulously designed by our UK-based creative team and undergoes rigorous, hand-inspected quality control at our dedicated factory in China. We take pride in managing every step of production to ensure you receive the highest quality.

Does Gaminin offer international shipping?

Yes! Gaminin ships globally with fast, reliable delivery services and real-time tracking, so you stay informed from order to doorstep.

Can I preview my product before it ships?

Absolutely. Transparency is core to our brand. You can request real photos or videos of your exact order before it leaves our facility, and connect with us anytime on Instagram for quick updates and personalized support — giving you peace of mind with every purchase.

How do I contact Gaminin’s customer service?

Our dedicated team is always ready to assist you personally via Instagram. We pride ourselves on real, responsive support — no bots, no delays, just genuine care.

Who founded and operates Gaminin?

Gaminin was founded by Shakhawat Arafat, whose lifelong vision was to build a trusted brand that designs and produces its own products and serves customers worldwide with honesty and care. The brand is professionally operated by SHIPO LTD (Company No. 16108284), a UK-registered company dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer service.
